Use index.html to start up the game in a monitor with >= 1680 x 1050.
Hit F11 to go fullscreen.
Use arrows to move Hero1.  (Hero2 is w,z,s,a; Hero3 is r,c,f,d)

How to play:
1) don't let a red-eyed monster or zombie get close to you!
2) touch a monster (no red eyes) to make him your red-eyed monster.
   He will be yours until he hits a wall.  He will kill any zombie he touches.
   Also, he will hurt any other Hero.
3) Game ends when all zombies are dead.
